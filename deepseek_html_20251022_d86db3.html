About Us

Kyambogo University Demography & Reproductive Health Association is a registered students association of Kyambogo University uniting students, alumni and professionals in the field of population studies and Reproductive Health. We focus on impactful research, Reproductive Health advocacy, Monitoring and Evaluation, Project Management, Data Analysis and Policy Formulation. As Systems theory states, we believe in a holistic approach Population and reproductive health approaches which can be achieved through partnerships. We are open to partnerships and Volunteer activities.

Founded in 2019, our association has been at the forefront of student-led initiatives that have shaped campus life and policies. We organize events, provide resources, offer career guidance sessions and serve as the voice of under privileged students.

Our mission is to empower students, foster a sense of community, and create an inclusive environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and personally.
